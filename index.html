Slogan

At Rift Lake Safaris we pride ourselves in being citizens of a country with amazing wildlife and natural features. A big feature among them being The Great Rift Valley! Aside from the beuty of the escarpments, within this valley lies six lakes, with differences and simililarities influenced by surrounding environments.

At Rift Lake Safaris we offer you an opportunity to tour all these Lakes, complete with great experineces including:

  • Boat rides
  • Free photography
  • Tour of surrounding towns
  • Local cultures and cuisines

The Safari takes a period of twelve days, inclusive of travel days! We offer a complete package so you do not have to worry about a thing. Our package includes:

  1. Transportation, including pick up and drop off
  2. Accomodation in all the towns toured
  3. Sunglasses and caps
  4. Toiletry bags with a toothbrush, toothpaste, and a menstrual hygiene care bag

Among the team we have a first-aider trained by Red Cross Kenya, and five fully equipped first-aid kits in case of any emergencies. We are compliant with the Covid-19 guidlines from the Ministry of health, and are insistent that our clients are as well. In this regard, you will be required to

  • Wear a face mask at all times.
  • Observe social distance.
  • Sanitize as frequently as possible.
  • Agree to frequent tenperature checks.
  • Inform a member of the team in case you are not feeling well.
